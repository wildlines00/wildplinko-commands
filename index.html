Wild Plinko

Chat Commands

Joining & Playing

!plinko
Join the active game session.
!drop
Drop your ball onto the board.

Locking In

!redeem
Lock in your current points. No more drops allowed after redeeming.
!gamble
Risk your max win on the Wild Plinko board. Only available at 1000pts.

Special Board Pegs

When the session locks, 4 unique glowing pegs spawn dynamically across the middle rows of the board. Physically pass your ball through one first to steal its effect and weaponize it against other chatters using the active target commands below!

💰

!steal [username]

Steals 25% of your targeted opponent's point pool and adds it straight to yours.

🧲

!magnet [username]

Adds a magnetic pull toward the closest death zone.

🔻

!shrink [username]

Shrinks the target's next dropped ball 45% of its regular scaling size.

⬇️

!gravity [username]

Increases the weight of victims ball 2.2× faster down the board.

Collectible Rules:

  • Retention: Items carry over safely between drops until you use them. Holding status shows up as a status badge right inside the leaderboard.
  • Respawn Rates: Once an item peg is struck and claimed, it will vanish and respawn on a completely fresh, peg after 20 seconds.
  • Execution: Target attacks trigger instantly on the victim's immediate next !drop.

Power-Ups

*Must be purchased before dropping, unless specified otherwise.

!bounce
50 pts
Increases your ball's bounce intensity on the pegs.
!doubleball
100 pts
Drops two balls simultaneously instead of one.
!buyjump
200 pts
Purchases the Jumper utility. (Activate mid-air using !jump)
!jump
In-flight
Activates the Jumper mid-drop to bounce the ball upwards. Must buy !buyjump first.
!nodeath
500 pts
Replaces the 2 outermost death zones with 25× multipliers.
!chaos
600 pts
Randomizes all bucket values for 30 seconds. Affects every player on the board!

The Rules

  • Power-ups can be stacked together on the exact same drop.
  • Purchases are only valid if they leave you with at least 1pt remaining.
  • No power-up purchases allowed once you reach max win (1000pts).
  • When using !doubleball, death only triggers if both balls land in death zones.
